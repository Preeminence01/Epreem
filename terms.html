1. Marketplace Role

EPREEM operates as a marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of high-value goods. EPREEM facilitates listings, payments and communication but is not the seller of record for third-party listings unless explicitly stated.

2. Seller Obligations

Sellers agree to list only items they have the legal right to sell, to describe condition and provenance accurately, and to honor auction results and confirmed orders in good faith.

3. Buyer Protections

Buyers may raise a dispute on any order that does not match its listing. EPREEM's resolution team reviews disputed orders and may withhold seller payouts pending resolution.

4. Fees & Commissions

EPREEM charges a category-based commission on completed sales, deducted from seller payouts. Current rates are visible to sellers in their dashboard.

5. Data & Privacy

EPREEM collects account, listing and transaction data necessary to operate the marketplace. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Account Suspension

EPREEM may suspend accounts that violate these terms, including fraudulent listings, repeated disputes found in the buyer's favor, or failure to complete identity verification when requested.
