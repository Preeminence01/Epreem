How we protect you

Trust & Verification

Every part of EPREEM is designed around one question: would we trust this transaction with our own money?

Seller Verification

Individual sellers verify their identity before listing. Business accounts — jewelry stores, car dealerships, real estate agencies, hospitals and medical suppliers, equipment suppliers, art galleries — additionally submit business registration documents, which our admin team reviews before the business badge is granted.

Secure Payments

Checkout supports cards, mobile money, bank transfer and digital wallets. Funds are processed through encrypted, PCI-aware payment gateways — EPREEM never stores raw card numbers.

Dispute Resolution

If an order doesn't match its listing or a handover is delayed, buyers can raise a dispute directly from their order page. Our resolution team reviews the order history, messages and listing details before making a determination.

Auction Integrity

Every bid is timestamped and recorded permanently against the auction. Outbid notifications are sent immediately, and the full bid history is visible to anyone watching the lot.
