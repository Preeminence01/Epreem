Frequently Asked Questions

How does EPREEM verify sellers?

Individual sellers submit identity documents; business accounts also submit registration paperwork. Both are reviewed by our admin team before approval.

What happens if I'm outbid on an auction?

You'll receive an instant notification the moment someone places a higher bid, so you can respond before the countdown ends.

What payment methods are supported?

Credit/debit cards, mobile money, bank transfer and digital wallets.

My order arrived differently than described. What now?

Open the order from your profile and select "Report a Problem" to raise a dispute — our resolution team will review it.
